# Privacy Policy — Regex Tester

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Overview

Regex Tester is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension processes all regular expressions and test strings entirely within your browser and does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

## Data Access

Regex Tester does **not** access any browsing data, page content, or network activity. The extension only processes text that you explicitly type into its interface.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Pattern history | Quick access to recently used regex patterns | Local storage |
| Saved patterns | User-saved patterns with labels | Local storage |
| Preferences | Default flags, theme, history limit | Local storage |

**No data is stored on external servers.**

## Data Transmission

Regex Tester does **not** transmit any data to external servers. Specifically:

- No regex patterns or test strings are sent anywhere
- No analytics or telemetry are collected
- No browsing history is recorded or shared
- No personal information is transmitted to third parties
- No network requests are made by the extension

## Third-Party Services

Regex Tester uses **no** third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | Save pattern history and preferences locally |

Regex Tester requests **minimal permissions** — it does not need access to tabs, browsing history, or any web content.

## Data Retention

Pattern history and saved patterns persist until you uninstall the extension, clear them manually, or they are evicted by the configured history limit.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
